Apex VoiceOrder
New · Launching 2026

AI-powered voice ordering platform for restaurants. Answers every inbound call 24/7, takes orders conversationally in American, Indian, and Pakistani English accents, processes card payment on the call via secure keypad entry, and routes confirmed orders directly to the restaurant's POS system. Built specifically for the underserved US South Asian restaurant segment — a $540M addressable market.
